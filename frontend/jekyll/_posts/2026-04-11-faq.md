# Cyberia FAQ

## What is Cyberia?
Cyberia is an experimental ecosystem built around its own EVM L1 blockchain.

It combines:
- a native blockchain (Cyberia)  
- tokens (CYBER, TG, CYBER.sol)  
- a Telegram-based onboarding system  

The goal is to build a self-sustaining network — not just a token.

---

## What is TG token?
TG is a token distributed through Telegram.

It is used to:
- incentivize activity in the chat  
- onboard users into Cyberia  
- distribute tokens early before the full ecosystem is live  

TG is not sold — it is earned.

---

## How do I start?
1. Join the chat  
2. Send: `/set_wallet <your EVM address>`
3. Receive TG every hour  

That’s it.

---

## What kind of wallet do I need?
You need an **EVM-compatible wallet** (like MetaMask).

Solana wallets are not supported for TG.

---

## I sent /set_wallet but nothing happened
If the bot didn’t respond, try again.

There was a temporary issue earlier, but it has been fixed.

Rewards are distributed hourly — delays can happen.

---

## Where are my tokens stored?
Your TG tokens are sent to your EVM address inside the Cyberia blockchain.

This is not Ethereum or Solana — it’s a separate network.

---

## Is this free?
Yes.

- No deposit required  
- No payment required  

You earn TG by participating.

---

## What is CYBER?
CYBER is the native coin of the Cyberia blockchain.

It is used for:
- transactions  
- network operations  
- future economic mechanics  

---

## What is CYBER.sol?
CYBER.sol is a separate token deployed on Solana.

It is used to:
- attract external liquidity  
- make Cyberia visible outside its own network  
- act as an entry point via platforms like pump.fun  

CYBER.sol is **not** a wrapped or pegged version of CYBER.

In the future, it will be used as a mechanism to bridge value into the Cyberia ecosystem.

---

## Are CYBER and CYBER.sol the same?
No.

- **CYBER** → native coin of the Cyberia blockchain  
- **CYBER.sol** → separate token on Solana  

They are **not 1:1 pegged** and have independent pricing.

CYBER.sol is an auxiliary token designed for distribution, liquidity, and exposure — especially through platforms like pump.fun.

Their connection is strategic, not technical (for now).

---

## How does everything connect?
Cyberia has three layers:

1. **Telegram layer** → onboarding (TG token)  
2. **Solana layer** → liquidity & exposure (CYBER.sol)  
3. **Cyberia L1** → core system (CYBER)  

Each layer has its role:
- TG → attracts users  
- CYBER.sol → attracts liquidity  
- CYBER → powers the network  

---
